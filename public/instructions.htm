Instructions Please read them carefully

This game is played by X human players, in the role of commuters who must reach their destination before 12:00 am. Two options are available:

  • Public Transport by Bus: 2 hours travel time, departure time is fixed: 10:00 am.
  • Car Sharing Service: 1 hour travel time, departure time between 10:00 and 11:00 am.
You will get a payoff for reaching your destination as close as possible to 12:00 am, and with as little travel time as possible. Therefore, you get the highest payoff when you arrive at 12:00 with one hour of travel time by car. However, the number of available cars is limited!

The earlier you arrive to the car sharing site, the higher is the chance to find a car available. However, the advantage of the shorter travel time by car is reduced because you will reach your destination too early.

If you arrive to the car sharing site and there are no cars left, you are forced to take the next bus and you will arrive with an according delay.
