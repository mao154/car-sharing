Instructions Please read them carefully

This game is played by X human players, in the role of commuters who must reach their destination before 12:00PM. Two options are available:

  • Public Transport by Bus: 2 hours travel time, departure time fixed: 10:00AM.
  • Car Sharing Service: 1 hour travel time, departure time flexible (10:00 -11:00AM).
You will get a payoff for reaching your destination as close as possible to 12:00PM, and with as little travel time as possible.

Notice! The number of cars available at the car sharing site is limited. Therefore the earlier you arrive to the car sharing site, the higher is the chance to find a car available. However, the advantage of the shorter travel time by car is reduced if you reach your destination too early.

Payoff Computation

  • Taking the car at 10:00AM, therefore arriving one hour early, gives you X% of the maximum payoff.
  • Taking the car at 11:00AM, therefore arriving to destination at 12:00PM with 1 hour travel time, gives you the maximum payoff.
  • Taking the car between 10:00 and 11:00AM gives you a payoff between X% and 100% of the maximum payoff.
  • If you arrive to the car sharing site and there are no cars available, you are forced to take the next bus. You will arrive with an according delay and with a payoff between 0 and X% of the maximum payoff.
  • Taking the bus at 10:00AM always gives you X% of the maximum payoff.
