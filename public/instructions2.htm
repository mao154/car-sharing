Bonus Conversion

The game is repeated X rounds, in each of which you can earn up to a maximum payoff of 100 ECU. Your final bonus is equal to the sum of payoffs collected in each round.

ECU are converted to US dollars with a factor of X to 1, so that you need to collect X ECU to win 1 dollar.

Final Guidelines

Attention! To ensure that you receive your pay:
  • Do not use the "Back Button" of your browser during the game.
  • Do not open multiple tabs.
  • If your browser crashes or your connection goes down, you can reconnect any time until the game is finished. All the rounds that you don't play will not be added to your final payoff.
  • You need to play at least 50% of the rounds and be connected during the last round to receive your payment.
